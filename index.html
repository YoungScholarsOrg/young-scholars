01 — About

What we're building

Young Scholars is a student-led educational initiative that brings together students and experts through free online sessions, workshops, and discussions. We exist because the most useful conversations about money, law, mental health, technology, and careers often happen too late — we want students to have them now, led by people willing to share what they actually know.

Summer School · 2025

Young Scholars' Summer School 2025

Young Scholars' Summer School 2025 was a three-day online learning experience designed to introduce students to new academic fields and career pathways through engaging, interactive sessions. The programme received 21 registrations from students across India and featured sessions led by volunteer instructors in Law and Psychology. Beyond lectures, the summer school emphasized participation and community-building through quizzes, discussions, and fun activities that encouraged students to actively engage with the material and with one another. Participants explored key concepts in both subjects, gained insights into potential career paths, and had the opportunity to ask questions directly to instructors. The programme demonstrated the potential of student-led educational initiatives to create accessible learning opportunities and meaningful conversations beyond the traditional classroom. Young Scholars' Summer School 2025 laid the foundation for future Young Scholars events and inspired the continued growth of the initiative.
