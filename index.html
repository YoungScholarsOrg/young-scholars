01 — About

What we're building

Young Scholars is a student-led educational initiative that brings together students and experts through free online sessions, workshops, and discussions. We exist because the most useful conversations about money, law, mental health, technology, and careers often happen too late — we want students to have them now, led by people who know what they're talking about.

Previous sessions

Summer School · June 23–25, 2025

Young Scholars' Summer School 2025

Young Scholars' Summer School 2025 was a three-day online learning programme that introduced students to the fields of Law and Psychology through interactive sessions led by volunteer instructors. The programme received 20+ student registrations and included quizzes, discussions, and fun activities designed to make learning engaging and collaborative. Students had the opportunity to explore new academic disciplines, interact directly with instructors, and connect with fellow learners from different backgrounds.
